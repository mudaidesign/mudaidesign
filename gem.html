Gem Shopping Network™

Enhancing E-Commerce for Maximum Impact

Gem Shopping Network™ is a high-end jewelry retailer with a 24-hour cable network dedicated to showcasing one-of-a-kind pieces. With a reach of more than 40 million U.S. households, the network showcases high-quality, affordable gemstones and fine jewelry from around the world.

In late 2015, Gem Shopping Network partnered with MU/DAI to redesign their e-commerce website, with the goal of increasing revenue generated from the website and enhancing the overall digital experience. To ensure a site launch in time for the holidays, we created a streamlined plan designed for maximum impact in a short time.

Our work began with stakeholder interviews, brand evaluation, competitive assessment and an analysis of the current website. Together with the client team, we rearticulated brand attributes and redeveloped the organization’s logo, typefaces, color palette and core interface elements.

With the design direction established, we applied the new look and feel across the existing site. In parallel with visual design, we created screen flows and wireframes defining new interactive features, using an iterative approach to quickly solidify longer-term enhancements such as product categorization, advanced filtering and personalized shopping tools.

This approach enabled us to get to market quickly with a visual refresh while simultaneously defining the new features that would be added to the site over a short series of subsequent releases.

Over the course of a few short weeks, we were able to deliver a suite of fully responsive templates as well as a collection of new features. Our work launched just before Black Friday and resulted in increases in time spent on site, conversion and revenue generated from the site.

This initial launch has set the stage for further site evolution and enhancements that Gem Shopping Network will continue to launch throughout 2016 and beyond.
